Short Bio

Since my bachelor's studies, I have developed a strong interest in artificial intelligence, agents, and multi-agent systems. I find challenging the modeling of interaction between autonomous agents and their use in software engineering. At the same time, I am really interested in the complex relation between AI and ethics.

I got my M. Sc. Degree in Computer Science in October 2017 at the University of Torino, with a final mark of 110/110 cum laude and special mention and a PhD in Computer Science at the same university in 2021. In December 2017 I graduated at the Scuola di Studi Superiori "Ferdinando Rossi" of the University of Torino, with a final mark of 70/70 cum laude.

My PhD thesis, written under the supervision of Professor Matteo Baldoni, deals with the problem of building robust multi-agent systems. It proposes an exception handling mechanism for use in MAS, built upon the notions of responsibility and reliable feedback.
